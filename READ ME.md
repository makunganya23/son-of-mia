# Disc Sales System

Mfumo wa usimamizi wa mauzo wa bidhaa za disc (cutting discs) unaotumia teknologia ya HTML, CSS, na JavaScript.

## Features
- ✅ Mfumo wa kuingia na kusajili
- ✅ Usimamizi wa bidhaa
- ✅ Point of Sale (POS)
- ✅ Usimamizi wa watumiaji
- ✅ Ripoti za mauzo
- ✅ Mipangilio ya mfumo

## Demo Accounts
- **Super Admin**: admin@discsales.com / admin123
- **Cashier**: cashier@discsales.com / cashier123

## Teknolojia
- HTML5
- CSS3 (Glass morphism design)
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Bootstrap 5.3.2
- Font Awesome 6.0.0
- LocalStorage for data persistence
